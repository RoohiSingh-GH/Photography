Camera Photography

There are four main types of digital camera: compact, bridge, DSLR and mirrorless cameras. DSLRs and mirrorless models have interchangeable lenses.
The type you go for will depend on a number of factors, including what you want to photograph or film, how portable you want it to be and the image quality you're looking for.
Things to Consider while Camera Photography :
1.Aperture.
2.Shutter Speed.
3.ISO.
4.Exposure

Phone Photography

Smartphone cameras are nowhere near as powerful as DSLRs, and even some point-and-shoots.The megapixel count is low, and they lack the manual controls that let you achieve shallow depth of field and super-crisp shots of subjects in motion. And the editing apps are nothing compared to programs like Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop
Things to Consider while Phone Photography :
1.Use Gridlines to balance shot.
2.Set your camera focus.
3.Focus on one subject.
4.Find different perspective.
5.Play with reflection.
6.Look for symmetry.
